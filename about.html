About Us

We provide adventurous experiences and themed camps for adults in beautiful Finnish landscapes and delightful nature spots where you can escape reality and take a rest. You have the possibility to totally unwind from everyday life and experience an amazing region in Finland.

With immersive experiences, committed and well-trained staff, in a safe and monitored space, we aspire to deliver to you one of the best life experiences.
